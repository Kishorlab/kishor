Research Publications

BSc Thesis (2025)

Topographical Influence on Chemical Properties of Forest Soil in Karnali Province

This research examined how altitude and aspect influence soil organic carbon, total nitrogen, available phosphorus, potassium and pH across different elevation zones in Bhairam Community Forest, Surkhet. Statistical analysis using linear mixed-effects modeling revealed significant impacts of aspect and altitude on SOC and TN, highlighting topographic influence on carbon sequestration and soil fertility in Himalayan ecosystems.
